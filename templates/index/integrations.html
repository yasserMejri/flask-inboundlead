Integrations

Scribe integrates into your existing workflow and CRM, allowing you to focus on more important tasks.
Schedule A Demo

Salesforce

Bi-Directional Sync: Automatically sync fields, status, and more on Leads, Contacts, and Accounts. Create records between Salesforce and Scribe, either bi-directional or one-way.

Activity Sync: Log all activity done by Scribe on Salesforce.

Salesforce Apps: Work in conjunction with Outreach and any other tools you use today.

  

Google

Scribe integrates with your Google calendar to identify which time slots are available to book a demo or call with your qualified lead.

Once a demo is booked, your lead and you both get a calendar invite and personal reminders before the call.

Scribe also integrates with Google Drive to share all lead information if you are too lazy to provide us with a webhook

  

Webhook

Scribe also sends all the relevant lead information and status of the lead to a webhook of your choice.

Scribe maintains records and syncs with your favorite choice of CRMs as well as supports free Google Spreadsheet data integration

In addition to the above, if you want to sync your lead information with your api endpoint, just provide us with corresponding webhook url and you are all set
